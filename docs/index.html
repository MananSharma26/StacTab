What is StacTab

Tab chaos is a solved problem.

StacTab is a lightweight Chrome extension that brings order to your browser automatically.

  • ⚡ Auto-grouping: Watches your tabs in real time and groups tabs by domain name automatically the moment your threshold is crossed. No configuration needed.
  • 🔍 Instant search: Hit Alt+S to search every open tab by title or URL in real time. Alt+G to instantly group all tabs by domain.
  • 🗄️ Workspace Vault: Archive entire sessions and restore them anytime. Never lose a tab again.

Built for anyone who's ever had 20+ tabs open and lost track of what they were doing.

Workspace Vault

Your sessions. Safe, searchable, forever.

The Workspace Vault is StacTab's most powerful feature: a tab archive that lives entirely in your browser.

  • 📛
    Named Sessions Save groups of tabs as "Work", "Research", "Shopping", whatever makes sense to you.
  • 📦
    Flexible Archive Options Archive the current tab, all tabs, tabs to the left or right, or an entire tab group in one action.
  • 🪟
    Split Groups to Windows Explode any tab group into its own new Chrome window, perfect for deep focus sessions.
  • ♻️
    Smart Restore Restore individual tabs, all tabs from a domain, all tabs from a day, or an entire session in a new window.
  • ↩️
    30-Second Undo Accidentally archived the wrong thing? A countdown undo button lets you instantly reverse it.
  • 📤
    JSON & TXT Export Export your entire vault as structured JSON for backup or portability, or as a plain .txt reading list.
